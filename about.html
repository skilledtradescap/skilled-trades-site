About Skilled Trades Growth Capital

Skilled Trades Growth Capital is an emerging, Maryland-focused initiative exploring how to bring flexible, revenue-based financing to established local trade businesses. The goal is simple: help strong HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, and home-service companies grow without forcing owners to sell equity or rely solely on rigid bank debt.

Why skilled trades, and why Maryland?

Skilled-trade businesses are essential to the functioning of every community. They keep homes heated and cooled, water running, electrical systems safe, and properties maintained. In Maryland, demographic trends, electrification, and infrastructure needs are all contributing to strong, sustained demand for these services.

At the same time, many trade businesses struggle to access growth capital that matches the way their businesses actually work. Asset-light models, uneven paperwork, and seasonal revenue patterns do not always fit neatly into conventional lending criteria—even when the underlying business is healthy.

Our approach

Rather than starting with a large fund or complex structure, this initiative is being developed gradually and carefully, focusing first on:

  • • Understanding the needs and constraints of Maryland trade owners
  • • Testing simple, transparent revenue-based structures
  • • Maintaining clear alignment between owners and investors
  • • Keeping documentation and process as straightforward as possible

Over time, the aim is to refine a model that works consistently for both sides and can potentially be scaled or replicated, while keeping a clear focus on real-economy impact: stronger local businesses, better-quality jobs, and more resilient Maryland communities.

Where things stand today

At this stage, Skilled Trades Growth Capital is an exploratory effort rather than a fully launched fund. Any potential transaction would be evaluated individually, and any investment opportunities would be offered only in compliance with applicable laws and to appropriately qualified investors.

Nothing on this site should be understood as a commitment to provide financing or as an offer or solicitation to buy or sell any security.

Growth capital that moves with your revenue, not against it.

Most HVAC, plumbing, electrical and home-service companies have more demand than capacity. We provide flexible capital so you can hire, add trucks, and upgrade tools—without selling equity or taking on stressful fixed loan payments.

What we provide

Growth capital

$50,000–$200,000 for established Maryland trade businesses to fund hiring, trucks, equipment, and expansion.

Revenue-based repayment

You repay a small percentage of monthly revenue (typically 5–8%) until a pre-agreed cap is reached.

No equity or control

You keep 100% ownership and full control of how you run and grow your company.

Why it fits skilled trades

Skilled-trade businesses are built around recurring work, reputation, and crews—not just hard collateral. Traditional bank loans often don’t reflect this reality. Our model is designed for companies that:

Have a track record

2–3+ years in business, with consistent revenue and a base of repeat customers or maintenance plans.

Are profitable or close

Positive or near-positive cash flow, where the main constraint is working capital and capacity, not demand.

Know how to grow

You have a clear plan: more technicians, more trucks, new service areas, or equipment that unlocks bigger jobs.

How a typical structure might look

Exact terms depend on the specifics of your business, but a common pattern is:

  • • Investment: $100,000
  • • Monthly repayment: 6% of revenue
  • • Repayment cap: 1.4x (total $140,000)
  • • Expected duration: 18–36 months, depending on revenue

When your revenue is strong, you repay faster. When it’s slower, the payment adjusts down automatically. This helps protect your cash flow while you grow.

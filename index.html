How the financing works

Instead of a fixed bank loan, you repay a small percentage of your monthly revenue (typically 5–8%) until a set total amount is reached. When revenue is high, you repay faster. When revenue slows, your payment adjusts automatically.

1. Capital

We invest $50,000–$200,000 into established, cash-flowing Maryland skilled-trade businesses.

2. Repayment

You share a small percentage of monthly revenue until a pre-agreed cap (e.g. 1.3–1.6x) is repaid.

3. Flexibility

No equity, no board, and payments that move with your revenue — protecting your cash flow.

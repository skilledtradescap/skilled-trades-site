How our model works

Sector focus

Maryland HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, and home-service companies with steady revenue and clear growth opportunities.

Revenue-based financing

Capital is repaid via a small share of monthly revenue until a pre-agreed cap is reached, aligning repayment with cash flow.

Deal-by-deal structure

Investors participate through specific transactions, and owners retain full equity and operational control of their companies.

Who this is for

Trade owners

Owners with 2–3+ years of operating history, reliable revenue, and a concrete plan for how additional capital would drive growth.

Investors

Investors interested in a niche, illiquid, higher-risk private credit strategy tied to real, local businesses and tangible services.

Local ecosystem

Maryland communities that benefit from better-financed trades: faster service, more jobs, and stronger local business networks.
